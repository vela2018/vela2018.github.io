Hola! My name is Jorge A. Vela, and I am your candidate for city commissioner of El Cenizo, Texas. Although I love speaking more about the issues than about myself, some vital information to note is that I am 26 years old, have a bachelor’s degree in mass communication from Texas State University, and always root for my favorite soccer teams of Pachuca and Houston Dynamo each weekend.

Also, I love politics because of the endless opportunities and things that can be completed if things are done the right way and the people are engaged in the process as well. As I did in my previous campaign for city commissioner eight years ago at the age of 18, I want to once again humbly ask for your support. Although this time I want to serve YOU as a write-in candidate, it is important to understand that this is so I can get to meet and know most of you and formulate a bond that does not just create the opportunity to serve as your commissioner but also a movement that propels more people to get involve in local politics. Vamos por todo!
